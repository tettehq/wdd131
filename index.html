About Me

My name is Aaron, but most of my friends call me Tetteh (pronounced "teh-teh"). I am a Ghanaian, and I currently live in a coastal town called Kpone. The indigens have dubbed the town "Kpone-On-Sea" to emphasize that its closeness to the sea. I am an online student majoring in Software Development, and I work as a math and science tutor. I love computers so much, and I am always enthusiastic to learn something new about them. I think family is one of the most important gifts we have been blessed with, and I love mine dearly.

Ghana

With a population of about 35 million, Ghana is the second most populous nation in West Africa. It is an anglophone nation bordered on three sides by francophone countries, and to the south by the Atlantic ocean. There are numerous tourist attractions in the country, and an interesting fact is that until Morocco who reached the 2022 FIFA World Cup semi-finals, Ghana was the only African country to go as far as quarter-finals in the 2010 editon of the competition.
